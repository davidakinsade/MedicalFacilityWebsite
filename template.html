AccessPay is the UK’s leader in innovative bank integration solutions

Designed to modernise banking operations processes, minimise the risk of fraud and error, enhance efficiency and optimise cash visibility for finance & treasury leaders, AccessPay is a driver of finance transformation for more than 1,000 customers worldwide.

Our mission

Making money move is at the core of what we do. We envision a world in which businesses can easily access the financial services & payments infrastructure required to power their growth, and in turn create economic opportunities for all.

Our innovative, flexible, and secure bank integration & data transformation platform is the foundation on which a more connected and transparent financial ecosystem is built.

AccessPay timeline & milestones

 

2024 | AccessPay Closes $24 Million Strategic Funding Round

AccessPay announces a $24 Million strategic funding round led by True Ventures, with participation from Mastercard, NatWest, Beringea, Route 66 Ventures, and Northern VC Praetura Ventures.

 

2023 | AccessPay launches Fraud & Error Prevention Suite

AccessPay launch the Fraud & Error Prevention Suite with features like Confirmation of Payee, Sanctions Screening, and Payment Screening, enabling customers to access bank-grade security tools for ultimate peace of mind when processing transactions.

The same year, a premium integration with Sage is announced which enables Sage customers, who are predominantly based in the US, to seamlessly retrieve their bank statements to enable auto-reconciliation within Sage Intacct.

 

2022 | AccessPay and Finastra announce collaboration

AccessPay announce their partnership with global provider of financial software applications and marketplaces Finastra, bringing the ease of seamless bank integration to corporates worldwide.

 

2021 | AccessPay wins at the PayTech Awards 2021

AccessPay wins at the PayTech Awards 2021, presented by FinTech Futures.

 

2014 | Anish Kapoor joins AccessPay as CEO

Anish Kapoor joins AccessPay as CEO, bringing his extensive experience of building high-performing teams and successful technology solutions into the fledgling business.

 

2012 | AccessPay is founded by Ali Moiyed

AccessPay is founded in Manchester by Ali Moiyed as the first cloud-based BACS software in the United Kingdom.
